1. Register

Create an account with a username + email + password. Pick a country (you'll get a BOBAI in that national jersey as your avatar once the illustrator delivers). Email is used only for confirmation + password reset — never shown publicly, never spammed.

Before the account is created you'll see a short security briefing (anti-scam, anti-phishing, game disclaimer) that you have to acknowledge. After confirming, a confirmation link is sent to your inbox — click it once and you're in.

Optionally link a BEP-20 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Binance Web3, hardware — any wallet works). You just paste the address — no signing, no app install, no gas. The wallet is only needed if you want to be eligible for prize-pool payouts. You can play purely for fun without one — your username will still appear on the leaderboard, just without the ⚽ icon.

A linked wallet is permanent and unique: it cannot be changed or removed after linking, and the same wallet cannot be used on two different accounts. Double-check the address before confirming.

6. Prize pool

Where the BOBAI comes from

Two sources feed the prize-pool wallet (0x5E41…D105):

  • Token tax auto-flow: from 11 Jun 2026, 00:01 UTC until the Final (19 Jul 2026, 23:59 UTC), 0.52 % of every BOBAI buy/sell tax flows into the prize-pool wallet automatically — 0.26 % redirected from the BOB-burn slice + 0.26 % from the creator slice. The 3 % on-chain tax rate stays fixed; only the off-chain distribution changes for the WC26 window. After the Final, the split reverts to the standard 1 % BOBAI burn · 1 % BOB burn · 1 % creator.
  • Donations: anyone can send BNB or USDT (BEP-20) to the prize-pool wallet. Incoming funds are auto-swapped to BOBAI via PancakeSwap and added on-chain. Any amount welcome — see below.

Everything is verifiable on BscScan. The pool figure displayed on the leaderboard reads the actual on-chain BOBAI balance — there's nothing in a back-office database we could fudge.

Pool split

How each incoming BOBAI gets allocated depends on the tournament phase at the moment it arrives:

  • During group-stage matches: 60 % → Group Pot · 30 % → End Pool · 10 % → Crypto Pot
  • From R32 onwards: 90 % → End Pool · 10 % → Crypto Pot (Group Pot stops growing — it's paid out before KO starts)

Within each sub-bracket, the share is split equally among its members — every group 1st gets the same slice of the 55 %, every group 2nd gets the same slice of the 30 %, and every best-3rd gets the same slice of the 15 %. The bracket weights still guarantee that being 1st always pays more than being 2nd, and being 2nd always pays more than just sneaking into best-3rd.

Flat split inside the bracket = no “winner-take-most” effect; finishing 1st of your group with 30 group_points pays the same as finishing 1st with 60. The only thing that matters is which position you finished.

Crypto Pot (10 %)

Paid one day after the Final, alongside the End Pool. Split into 3 equal slots:

  • 3.3 % of total pool to the closest BTC guess
  • 3.3 % of total pool to the closest BNB guess
  • 3.3 % of total pool to the closest BOBAI guess

The same player can win multiple Crypto Pot slots if they have the best guess on more than one coin.

Eligibility

Only players with a linked wallet at payout time receive transfers. Players without a linked wallet keep their leaderboard position for bragging rights, but their share is redistributed proportionally to the eligible winners in that pot.

Holder-balance cap: max 26× your BOBAI

Each individual payout to a winner is capped at 26× the BOBAI balance held in their linked wallet at the moment of payout. Examples:

Your wallet holdsMaximum single payout
$1 in BOBAI$26
$10 in BOBAI$260
$100 in BOBAI$2,600
$1,000 in BOBAI$26,000
$10,000 in BOBAI$260,000
Why this exists. This is an anti-abuse rule that protects the people who actually believe in BOBAI / Brain On BNB AI — the holders, supporters, and long-term believers. Without a cap, anyone could spin up an empty wallet, place a random tip, and try to siphon a pool that was built by real supporters. The 26× rule ties payouts to actual skin in the game.

Don't worry about being "capped out". Nobody knows in advance how big the prize pool will be — but if the pool grows, your BOBAI balance grows with it (same token, same chart). So the cap scales with you. Holding more BOBAI literally raises your ceiling.

The balance is read live from the blockchain at payout time — there's no snapshot to game and no way to fake it.

Cap overflow — cascading redistribution

If a winner's computed share exceeds their personal 26× cap, the excess does not stay with that winner and is not kept by the team. It flows to the rest of the eligible winners in the same pot. Each pot (Group, End Pool, and the three Crypto pots) is evaluated independently — overflow never crosses pot boundaries.

  1. Compute every winner's raw share based on points / closest-guess logic.
  2. Apply each winner's 26× cap. Sum up the overflow from anyone who got capped.
  3. Redistribute that overflow proportionally to the remaining (uncapped) winners within the same pot.
  4. If that redistribution pushes a new winner over their cap, repeat step 2 — the cascade continues until either every dollar is paid out within someone's cap, or no eligible uncapped winner remains.
  5. If after the full cascade residual still remains (every winner in that pot is capped out), the leftover BOBAI is added as permanent liquidity to the BOBAI/BNB PancakeSwap pool, and the resulting LP tokens are burned to the dead address. It is never carried into another pot, never refunded, never kept by the team — the value flows back to every BOBAI holder through a deeper, tighter market.

The cascading logic is implemented in the same payout-computation code that the admin runs before signing. The full split (raw share, cap applied, redistribution rounds) will be published on-chain alongside each payout batch so anyone can verify it.

How the payouts run

Payout transactions are triggered manually by the project admin (not fully autonomous) — the code computes the exact split (including cap and cascade), then the admin signs and broadcasts the batch. This is a deliberate safety design: an off-by-one bug should never auto-drain the pool.

9. Security & manipulation safety

  • Tip lock: 15 minutes before kickoff, enforced at the database level — not just in the UI.
  • Bonus & crypto lock: enforced at the database level when the relevant match kicks off.
  • Match results: only the sync worker (using a service-role key, never exposed to users) can write results. Players cannot edit goals.
  • Scoring: runs in a database trigger after match results are written. The trigger overwrites any user-side `points` value, so nobody can self-award points.
  • Wallet linking: you just paste your BEP-20 address — no signing, no seed phrase, no private key. We never ask for any of that, and the app would never need it.
  • Wallet privacy: your linked wallet is never shown to other players in-app. The only public reveal is the on-chain payout transaction itself — and Group-Pot payouts are sent in randomized order across the 12 groups (e.g. group F may receive before group A), so observers cannot match a wallet to a specific group placement. Only the End-Pool top 4 (🥇/🥈/🥉/4th overall) and the three closest crypto guesses (BTC/BNB/BOBAI) are matchable to a wallet, because those are unique single-winner positions that are already public on the leaderboard.
  • Tie-breaker by BOBAI balance: read live from the blockchain at payout time, not from a database — un-fakeable.
  • Payouts: manually triggered by admin, not autonomous. An admin error or bug can be caught before signing.
  • Prize wallet: a fresh dedicated wallet. Private key is only stored in encrypted Cloudflare Worker secrets — never in code, never on a server you can SSH into.
Admins NEVER DM you first. Anyone offering you to "double your winnings", "verify your account", "claim your payout faster" via Telegram/X DM is a scammer. Block + report.
