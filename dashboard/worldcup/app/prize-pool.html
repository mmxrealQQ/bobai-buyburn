📋 How payouts work full audit logged on-chain + as PDF
Payouts happen twice: 1 day after group stage (Group pot) and 1 day after the Final (End pool + Crypto pot). Both runs follow the same transparent procedure — manually triggered by the admin, never auto-drained.
1
Snapshot leaderboard at lock time (last group match end, or Final end). All scores frozen. Bonus questions count toward total points.
2
Read wallet eligibility — only players with a linked BEP-20 wallet receive transfers. Unlinked players keep leaderboard position but their share redistributes to eligible winners.
3
Read on-chain BOBAI balance of each eligible wallet (live from blockchain, not from a database — un-fakeable). Used for the 26× holder cap + tie-breaker.
4
Compute raw shares per pot: Group: 55 % / 30 % / 15 % flat across 1st/2nd/best-3rd brackets. End pool: 50/25/15/10 % tiered for top 4. Crypto: 5 %/coin to closest guesses.
5
Apply 26× holder cap per winner. Excess flows to remaining uncapped winners proportionally. Cascade repeats until all dollars land within someone's cap or pot is exhausted.
6
Test payout: 1 % first. Admin signs a tiny test batch to verify addresses + amounts on-chain before full execution. If anything looks off, full payout aborts. No 99 % surprise.
7
Full payout in randomized group order (e.g. Group F before Group A) — so on-chain observers can't match wallets to group rank. Only End-Pool top 4 + 3 Crypto winners stay matchable, since those are unique single-winner positions already on the leaderboard.
8
Audit PDF published after each run. Lists every winner, raw share, cap applied, redistribution rounds, and final tx hash. Verifiable from this page.
💡 The same player can win in multiple groups (e.g. 1st in A + 2nd in D + best-3rd in F) — each placement is paid separately. Linked wallet is keyed to a username, not to a single group.
