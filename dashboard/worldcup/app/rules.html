1. Register

Create an account with a username + password. No email required. Pick a country (you'll get a $BOBAI in that national jersey as your avatar once the illustrator delivers).

Optionally connect a BEP-20 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Binance Web3). The wallet is only needed if you want to be eligible for prize-pool payouts. You can play purely for fun without one — your username will still appear on the leaderboard, just without the ⚽ icon.

Wallet ownership is verified via signature before any payout. We never ask for your seed phrase or private key — only an on-chain signature.

2. Bonus questions (one-time)

Five long-shot predictions, each worth 10 points. Lockable at the first match's kickoff:

  1. Who wins the World Cup? (single team)
  2. Team with the most goals scored across the tournament
  3. Team with the fewest goals scored
  4. Top scorer's country (Golden Boot)
  5. Total red cards bracket: <20 / 20–30 / 30–40 / 40–50 / >50

You can edit your answers any time before the first kickoff. After kickoff: locked — no changes.

3. Match tips

For every match you tip the exact score (e.g. 2:1). All 72 group-stage matches plus 32 knockout matches.

Each match locks 15 minutes before kickoff. After that: no edits possible (enforced at database level — you literally can't get around it).

Knockout matches start as TBD vs TBD in the UI and become tippable once the draw resolves them.

4. Crypto predictions

Predict the USD price of BTC, BNB and $BOBAI at the exact moment of the World Cup Final kickoff. Live current prices are shown for reference; you can edit anytime until kickoff.

Scoring is based on absolute difference to the actual price at the snapshot moment — the closest guess on each coin wins that coin's share of the crypto pot.

6. Prize pool

Where the BOBAI comes from

Two sources feed the prize-pool wallet (0x5E41…D105):

  • Token tax routing: starting 11 June 2026 (kickoff), 26 % of the 1 % creator buy/sell tax flows into the prize-pool wallet automatically. The remaining 74 % keeps funding the existing buyback bot and creator operations.
  • Donations: anyone can send BNB or USDT (BEP-20) to the prize-pool wallet. Incoming funds are auto-swapped to $BOBAI via PancakeSwap and added on-chain. Any amount welcome — see below.

Everything is verifiable on BscScan. The pool figure displayed on the leaderboard reads the actual on-chain BOBAI balance — there's nothing in a back-office database we could fudge.

Pool split

How each incoming BOBAI gets allocated depends on the tournament phase at the moment it arrives:

  • During group-stage matches: 45 % → Group Pot · 40 % → End Pool · 15 % → Crypto Pot
  • From R32 onwards: 85 % → End Pool · 15 % → Crypto Pot (Group Pot stops growing — it's paid out before KO starts)

7. Payouts

Group Pot (45 % of group-stage collection)

Paid one day after the last group-stage match. Split across 32 winners:

  • Top 2 finishers in each of the 12 groups (24 winners)
  • 8 best 3rd-placed finishers across all groups (8 winners)

Each player's share is proportional to their points within their winning bracket (top-2 or best-3rd). No fixed prizes — your share = your points ÷ sum of all qualifying players' points × bracket pool.

End Pool (KO-phase pot)

Paid one day after the Final. Top 4 overall — based on total score across all phases including bonus questions.

Crypto Pot (15 %)

Paid one day after the Final, alongside the End Pool. Split into 3 equal slots:

  • 5 % to the closest BTC guess
  • 5 % to the closest BNB guess
  • 5 % to the closest $BOBAI guess

The same player can win multiple Crypto Pot slots if they have the best guess on more than one coin.

Eligibility

Only players with a verified wallet at payout time receive transfers. Players without a connected wallet keep their leaderboard position for bragging rights, but their share is redistributed proportionally to the eligible winners in that pot.

How the payouts run

Payout transactions are triggered manually by the project admin (not fully autonomous) — the code computes the exact split, then the admin signs and broadcasts the batch. This is a deliberate safety design: an off-by-one bug should never auto-drain the pool.

8. Donations

The prize-pool wallet accepts BNB and USDT (BEP-20) on BNB Smart Chain. Address is shown on the Leaderboard page with a copy button.

BSC only. If you send USDT on Ethereum, Tron, Solana or any other network — funds are lost. There's no human in the loop to recover them.

How it works:

  1. You send X BNB or USDT to the prize wallet
  2. The pool sync worker detects the new balance
  3. BNB/USDT is auto-swapped to $BOBAI via PancakeSwap
  4. The new $BOBAI is added to the pool (verifiable on BscScan)
  5. Pool display updates within ~5 minutes

Any amount welcome. For gas efficiency we suggest at least the equivalent of ~$5, but anything works.

9. Security & manipulation safety

  • Tip lock: 15 minutes before kickoff, enforced at the database level — not just in the UI.
  • Bonus & crypto lock: enforced at the database level when the relevant match kicks off.
  • Match results: only the sync worker (using a service-role key, never exposed to users) can write results. Players cannot edit goals.
  • Scoring: runs in a database trigger after match results are written. The trigger overwrites any user-side `points` value, so nobody can self-award points.
  • Wallet verification: required before any payout. We never ask for your seed phrase or private key — only a signature.
  • Tie-breaker by BOBAI balance: read live from the blockchain at payout time, not from a database — un-fakeable.
  • Payouts: manually triggered by admin, not autonomous. An admin error or bug can be caught before signing.
  • Prize wallet: a fresh dedicated wallet. Private key is only stored in encrypted Cloudflare Worker secrets — never in code, never on a server you can SSH into.
Admins NEVER DM you first. Anyone offering you to "double your winnings", "verify your account", "claim your payout faster" via Telegram/X DM is a scammer. Block + report.
